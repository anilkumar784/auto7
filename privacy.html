Privacy Policy

At AutoZone Parts, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our website, purchase products, or contact us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you interact with our website:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping and billing address, and payment information when you place an order.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other analytics data via cookies or third-party tools like Google Analytics.
  • Communication Data: Information provided when you contact our support team, subscribe to newsletters, or fill out contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders.
  • Communicate with you about your orders or inquiries.
  • Send promotional offers and product updates (only if you opt in).
  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience.

3. How We Protect Your Data

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Payment data is processed securely through trusted third-party payment gateways and is not stored on our servers.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share it with trusted service providers (e.g., payment processors, shipping partners) solely for the purpose of fulfilling your order or improving our service. All third parties are required to safeguard your information.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our messages.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to provide essential functionality, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings. For more information, view our Cookies Policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
